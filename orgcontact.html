Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How long does it take to see results from digital marketing?

Results vary by service: PPC ads can show immediate results, SEO typically takes 3-6 months, social media growth is usually visible in 1-3 months. We provide detailed timelines during our consultation.

Do you work with businesses outside Bangalore?

Yes! While we're based in Bangalore, we serve clients across India and internationally. Most of our work is done remotely with regular virtual meetings and detailed reporting.

What's included in your digital marketing packages?

Our packages are customized based on your needs. Common inclusions are strategy development, implementation, ongoing optimization, monthly reporting, and dedicated account management. We'll create a custom package during consultation.

Do you provide contracts? What are your terms?

We offer both short-term projects and ongoing retainer agreements. Most of our clients prefer 6-12 month contracts for better results, but we're flexible based on your requirements. No long-term commitments required to start.

How do you measure and report on campaign performance?

We use industry-standard tools like Google Analytics, Google Ads, social media insights, and custom dashboards. You'll receive detailed monthly reports with key metrics, insights, and recommendations for improvement.

What makes DigiStrive Media different from other agencies?

Our "Digital Precision" approach combines data-driven strategies with creative execution. We focus on measurable results, transparent reporting, and building long-term partnerships rather than just delivering services.
